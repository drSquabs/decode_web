The Prequel Trilogy

The Force Awakens will be part of a new trilogy that will be released within the next decade.

The Force Awakens follows the events of Return of the Jedi after the fall of the Empire as a new force rises to take it's place. Kylo Ren, leader of the "First Order" will stop at nothing to finish what Darth Vader started.
